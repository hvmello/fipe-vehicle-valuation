Refinement — Round 3 (caching strategy & dropdown performance)

Make the cache smart, not just present. Hot/cold working set via bounded, frequency-aware Caffeine (no bulk prefetch); FIPE's monthly reference-table update treated as an active trigger to refresh prices; large model lists rendered instantly. Same gated protocol.
